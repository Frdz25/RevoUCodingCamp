Who We Are

A Voice for Indonesia's Vanishing Wildlife

JagaAlam is a non-profit organization dedicated to raising awareness and driving action to protect Indonesia's most endangered species. From the rainforests of Sumatra to the coral reefs of Raja Ampat, we work with local communities, researchers, and policymakers.

Our mission is simple: ensure that future generations can still witness the incredible biodiversity that makes Indonesia one of the world's most ecologically rich nations.
